Why Agent Skills?
LLM agents rely on modular procedural knowledge — SKILL.md files that augment capabilities at inference time without fine-tuning. Adopted by Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Codex, OpenClaw, Cursor, Copilot, and 30+ platforms. Curated Skills improve pass rates by +16.2pp on average, yet 37% of community Skills contain security flaws.
